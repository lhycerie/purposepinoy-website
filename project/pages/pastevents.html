Community Outreach

Pasong Buaya

Our team visited the Pasong Buaya community to conduct educational workshops and distribute essential supplies. This event brought together volunteers and community members in a meaningful exchange of knowledge, resources, and hope. Through interactive sessions and personal connections, we were able to make a lasting impact on the lives of local students and families.

Location: Pasong Buaya, Philippines
Participants: Placeholder

Impact Highlights

  • ✓ Reached over 100 students with educational materials
  • ✓ Distributed school supplies and hygiene kits
  • ✓ Conducted motivational workshops and activities

ROTARACT X PURPOSE PINOY

In partnership with Rotaract, we launched a community support initiative to provide micro-entrepreneurs and small business owners with resources and mentorship. This collaboration brought together young professionals and community members to foster economic empowerment and sustainable livelihoods in underserved areas.

Location: Local Community, Philippines
Partner: Rotaract Philippines

Impact Highlights

  • ✓ Supported local sari-sari store owners
  • ✓ Provided business mentorship and resources
  • ✓ Built lasting partnerships with community entrepreneurs
