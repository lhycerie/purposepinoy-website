Partnership

Thrift for Good & The HOPE Foundation

Our Thrift for Good initiative combines sustainable fashion with community impact. We organize group thrift trips to source clothing, sell curated pieces online to raise funds, and then donate unsold items to the HOPE Foundation. During our visit, members host a “free shopping” event, promoting dignity and confidence for women and girls healing from trauma. We will also conduct a basic perfume-making vocational workshop to equip them with hands-on skills.

Impact: Hosting "free shopping" events to promote dignity for women healing from trauma.

Outreach

Street Children Christmas Toy Drive

Spread joy this December with our road-trip style outreach! The Street Children Christmas Toy Drive is a hands-on initiative designed to bring festive cheer and inclusion to street children across selected locations. Funds raised from our Thrift for Good program will be used to purchase and distribute toys, ensuring every child receives a gift and experiences the holiday season with warmth.

Impact: Distributing toys and gifts to ensure every child experiences the warmth of the holidays.

Relief Ops

Red Cross PH - Typhoon Uwan Relief

We are committed to immediate relief efforts by collaborating with Red Cross Philippines in Mandaluyong. We are fundraising to provide essential goods to families affected by recent typhoons. Our focus is on practical assistance through food items (canned goods, instant noodles, bottled water) and vital hygiene kits (toothbrushes, toothpaste, sanitary napkins, etc.).

Impact: Providing essential food items (canned goods, rice) and vital hygiene kits.
